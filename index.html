In case of Illness

1
Inform your line manager and Loyco by email or phone on the first day of absence.
2
Send the medical certificate by post to your HR department if your absence lasts 3 days or more.
3
Send each new medical certificate to your HR by post in case of long-term absence.
4
 Inform Loyco by e-mail or phone once you are back at work.

In case of Accident

1
In case of work incapacity, inform your line manager
2
Ensure you contact Loyco to fulfill an Accident declaration to get a refund from the insurance company
3
Send the medical certificate by post to your HR department on the first day of absence.
4
Send each new medical certificate to your HR by post in case of long-term absence.
5
 Inform Loyco by e-mail or phone once you are back at work
